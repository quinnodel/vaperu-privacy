VaPeru — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

VaPeru is designed to be private by default.

What we collect

Nothing. VaPeru collects no personal data. It has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and makes no network requests. The app runs entirely on your device.

Your data stays on your device

Your itinerary and your purchase status are stored locally on your device only. They are never transmitted to us or to any third party. Uninstalling the app removes this data.

Purchases

The one-time "Full Altitude Plan" purchase is processed by Apple's App Store or Google Play. We never see or store your payment information. Those platforms handle the transaction under their own privacy policies.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Medical disclaimer

VaPeru provides general planning guidance based on published Wilderness Medical Society thresholds. It is not medical advice and not a substitute for professional medical care.

Contact
