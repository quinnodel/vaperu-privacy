VaPeru — Support

Help for your Peru altitude-sickness planner

VaPeru helps you plan your Peru trip around altitude-sickness (soroche) risk — fully offline.

Common questions

The app needs no internet — is that right?

Yes. VaPeru works 100% offline. All city, risk, and emergency data ships inside the app. You never need a signal, even high in the Andes.

I bought the Full Altitude Plan but don't see it.

Open the Risk tab and tap Restore Purchase on the unlock card. Purchases are tied to your Apple ID, so they restore for free on any device or after reinstalling.

How is my altitude risk calculated?

From published Wilderness Medical Society thresholds, applied to the sleeping elevation and day-to-day ascent of the itinerary you enter. It is planning guidance, not a diagnosis.

Is my data private?

Completely. VaPeru collects nothing, has no accounts, and makes no network requests. See our Privacy Policy.

Medical disclaimer

VaPeru is planning guidance based on published thresholds — not medical advice and not a substitute for professional care. If symptoms are severe (confusion, loss of coordination, or a wet cough), descend and seek help immediately.
